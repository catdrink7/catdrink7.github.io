Grading

All plays are to win 1 unit.

For example, a -200 play will be risking 2 units to win 1 unit, and +200 play will be risking 0.5 units to win 1 unit.

Adjusted Win Percentage

Win percentage is adjusted based on units won.

For example, a 50-50 record with +5.5 units would result in a 55% adjusted win percentage as it is the same as a 55-45 record consisting of all -110 plays.

Write-ups

There will be no write-ups on any released plays. This is to preserve the value of certain information and methods that I use.
