catdr.ink is a handicapping service providing predictions for the Overwatch League.

Grading

All plays are to win 1 unit.

For example, a -200 play will be risking 2 units to win 1 unit, and a +200 play will be risking 0.5 units to win 1 unit.

Adjusted Win Record

Record is adjusted based on units won.

For example, a 50-50 record with +5.5 units would display as 55-45 as it is the same as a 55-45 record consisting of all -110 plays.

Closing Line Value

Closing line value is determined by converting odds to implied win percentage.

All closing lines are from Pinnacle, as they have the highest limits available for eSports.

Write-ups

There will be no write-ups on any released plays. This is to preserve the value of certain information and methods that I may use to predict these games.

Legal

catdr.ink is not an online gambling website.

catdr.ink does not accept or place wagers.

All information provided by catdr.ink is intended to be used for legal sports trading only and only in areas where sports trading is allowed under their respective legal codes.
